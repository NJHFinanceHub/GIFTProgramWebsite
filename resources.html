Sweet Potato Recipes

Delicious and nutritious ways to prepare both sweet potato roots and leaves

Roasted Sweet Potatoes

Simple and delicious - roasted with olive oil, salt, and herbs. Perfect side dish that brings out natural sweetness.

Prep: 10 min | Cook: 30 min

Sweet Potato Soup

Creamy, hearty soup packed with vitamins. Great for feeding families and community gatherings.

Prep: 15 min | Cook: 25 min

Sweet Potato Porridge

Nutritious breakfast porridge perfect for children. Can be made with milk or water, sweetened naturally.

Prep: 5 min | Cook: 20 min

Sautéed Sweet Potato Leaves

Quick and nutritious greens sautéed with garlic and oil. High in protein and iron, similar to spinach.

Prep: 5 min | Cook: 10 min

Sweet Potato Leaf Stew

Traditional African stew with sweet potato leaves, tomatoes, and spices. Perfect with rice or fufu.

Prep: 10 min | Cook: 30 min

Sweet Potato Leaf Salad

Fresh, raw young leaves mixed with vegetables and light dressing. Rich in antioxidants and vitamins.

Prep: 15 min | Cook: 0 min
