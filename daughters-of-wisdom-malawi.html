About Daughters of Wisdom

The Daughters of Wisdom bring a holistic approach to their ministry in Malawi, recognizing that people need both material provision and spiritual care. They don't separate the two - both matter, both are essential.

In communities where poverty and hunger are daily struggles, the Daughters of Wisdom work alongside local Malawians to address practical needs - food, health, education - while also nurturing the spiritual life that gives people hope and resilience in difficult times.

Their approach reflects a deep understanding that you can't separate body and soul. When someone is hungry, you feed them. When someone is sick, you care for them. When someone needs hope, you walk with them. It's all part of serving the whole person.

Their Mission

To serve both the material and spiritual needs of their community in Malawi, recognizing that true well-being encompasses body, mind, and spirit.

GIFT's Partnership

GIFT supports Daughters of Wisdom by providing sweet potato farming resources that address the material needs of nutrition and food security in their community.

Areas of Service

Material Care

Addressing practical needs like food, health care, and resources that help communities thrive physically.

Spiritual Care

Providing pastoral support, faith formation, and spiritual guidance that nourishes hope and resilience.

Community Building

Strengthening the bonds that hold communities together through shared faith and mutual support.

Nourishing Body and Spirit

The Daughters of Wisdom understand that you can't preach hope to someone who's hungry. That's why GIFT's sweet potato program fits so naturally into their holistic ministry - it addresses the very real, very physical need for nutritious food.

Sweet potatoes provide critical Vitamin A and other nutrients that combat malnutrition in Malawi. They're easy to grow, don't require expensive inputs, and produce reliable harvests that families can count on.

When families have enough to eat, they're healthier, children can focus on school, and people have the energy and hope to engage in community and spiritual life. It all works together - just as the Daughters of Wisdom have always known.
