WYNNE FARM AREA HAITI

About Wynne Farm

In a country where deforestation has created devastating environmental challenges, Wynne Farm Ecological Preserve stands as a living classroom and sanctuary. They believe that protecting nature starts with education and hands-on experience.

The farm serves as both an ecological preserve and an educational center where Haitians learn sustainable agricultural practices, environmental stewardship, and the connection between caring for creation and caring for community.

Their work demonstrates that we all have the power to heal the Earth - one plant, one practice, one person at a time. It's about restoration, not just of the land, but of hope.

Their Mission

To preserve nature and teach that everyone has the power to heal the Earth through environmental protection and sustainable education.

GIFT's Partnership

GIFT integrates sweet potato farming into Wynne Farm's curriculum, teaching sustainable agriculture that nourishes both people and planet.

Programs & Impact

Environmental Education

Teaching students and community members about ecology, conservation, and sustainable practices through hands-on learning.

Habitat Preservation

Bringing together local communities to participate in reforestation and sustainable farming initiatives.

Sweet Potatoes: Good for People, Good for the Planet

Sweet potatoes fit perfectly into Wynne Farm's mission of environmental education and sustainable agriculture. Unlike many crops, sweet potatoes actually improve soil health as they grow, preventing erosion with their sprawling vines and root systems.

Through GIFT's partnership, Wynne Farm teaches students and visitors how to grow this resilient crop using sustainable methods - no expensive chemicals needed, just good practices that work with nature instead of against it.

It's a perfect example of what Wynne Farm teaches: you can feed your family and heal the earth at the same time. The two goals aren't in conflict - they support each other.
