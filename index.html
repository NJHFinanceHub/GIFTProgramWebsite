Our Mission

GIFT (Global Institute For Transformation) is addressing global hunger and poverty through sustainable sweet potato farming projects. We partner with churches in Haiti, Malawi, and future locations to provide communities with nutritious food, economic opportunities, and sustainable agricultural practices.

The Power of Sweet Potatoes

Sweet potatoes are a nutritional powerhouse, rich in Vitamin A and essential nutrients that combat childhood mortality, malnutrition, and preventable diseases.

Every year, 670,000 children under age 5 die from Vitamin A deficiency, and 525,000 children die from diarrhea-related causes. Sweet potatoes can help prevent both.
