Our Mission

The Global Institute For Transformation (GIFT) is a 501(c)(3) cross-denominational ministry that brings life-transforming material and spiritual solutions to people in the poorest parts of the world and in the U.S. Our mission is: Transforming hearts, lives and communities by helping people fan into flame the gift(s) of God, including helping people more humbly receive, reciprocate and regift the gifts of God.

We partner with churches in Haiti, Malawi, Philippines and the U.S. to provide communities with nutritious food, economic opportunities, and sustainable agricultural practices - while also sharing spiritual resources for evangelization and discipleship.

The Power of Sweet Potatoes

Sweet potatoes are a nutritional powerhouse, rich in Vitamin A and essential nutrients that combat childhood mortality, malnutrition, and preventable diseases.

Every year, 670,000 children under age 5 die from Vitamin A deficiency, and 525,000 children die from diarrhea-related causes. Sweet potatoes can help prevent both.

The Two Faith Pillars of GIFT & TRANSFORMATION

GIFT focuses on two essential, foundational faith concepts that could compel us to live life differently: More Grateful, TRANSFORMED and transforming the world

1. GIFT

Reflecting on, appreciating and celebrating everything and everyone that God has freely given us as a GIFT.

We RECEIVE and more fully appreciate God's many gifts in humble appreciation.

2. TRANSFORMATION

Allowing our God, who created us, to TRANSFORM our lives, so we can become more like Him; to live and love more like Jesus.

We RECIPROCATE to God by allowing the Holy Spirit to transform us, and REGIFT God's loving gifts to others to transform our world.

Vatican Recognition

The Vatican Dicastery For Promoting Integral Human Development's Laudato Si Action Platform recently published GIFT's document on these two faith pillars of GIFT and TRANSFORMATION as a "Resource" for their network of organizations concerned about the "Cry of the Poor" and the "Cry of the Earth."
