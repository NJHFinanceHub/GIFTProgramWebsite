Spiritual Ministry

Supporting Faith-Based Partners in Holistic Community Transformation

Transforming Lives Through Faith and Action

GIFT recognizes that true transformation addresses both the material and spiritual needs of communities. Our Spiritual Ministry project supports faith-based partners who integrate spiritual care with practical programs that serve the whole person.

Whether it's offering pastoral care, teaching values of stewardship and sustainability, or providing hope and dignity to those facing extreme poverty, our partners demonstrate that faith in action creates lasting change.

We support ministries that serve children living with HIV, orphans, widows, and vulnerable families by combining spiritual guidance with agricultural training, education, and healthcare.

Core Values

  • Holistic care addressing body, mind, and spirit
  • Dignity and hope for the most vulnerable
  • Teaching stewardship and care for creation
  • Building community and belonging
  • Faith-driven compassion in action
