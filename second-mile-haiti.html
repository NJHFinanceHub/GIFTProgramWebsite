SECOND MILE HAITI

About Second Mile Haiti

Second Mile Haiti focuses on creating sustainable solutions that address childhood malnutrition and poverty at their roots. They don't just treat symptoms - they equip families with the tools and knowledge to build healthier, more prosperous lives.

Their approach combines medical care, health education, and practical tools that families can use to improve their quality of life long-term. It's about creating lasting change, not just temporary relief.

By addressing root causes - lack of nutrition, limited healthcare access, and poverty - Second Mile Haiti helps families build resilience and self-sufficiency. They're walking the "second mile" with Haitian families, going beyond immediate needs to create lasting transformation.

Their Mission

To create sustainable solutions that address childhood malnutrition and poverty by empowering families with medical care, health education, and practical tools for self-sufficiency.

GIFT's Partnership

GIFT provides sweet potato farming resources that help families combat malnutrition with sustainable, nutrient-rich food production.

Programs & Services

Medical Care

Providing essential medical treatment for malnourished children and ongoing health monitoring for families.

Health Education

Teaching families about nutrition, hygiene, and preventive health practices that protect their children.

Practical Tools

Providing families with resources and skills for sustainable food production and income generation.

Fighting Malnutrition with Sweet Potatoes

Childhood malnutrition often stems from Vitamin A deficiency, which weakens immune systems and makes children vulnerable to preventable diseases. Orange-fleshed sweet potatoes are one of the best natural sources of Vitamin A available.

Through our partnership, Second Mile Haiti teaches families how to grow sweet potatoes in their own small plots. This means consistent access to nutritious food without depending on outside aid. Families learn to feed themselves - sustainably.

It's the kind of practical tool that creates real, lasting change. Children get the nutrition they need, families gain food security, and communities build resilience. That's the second mile in action.
