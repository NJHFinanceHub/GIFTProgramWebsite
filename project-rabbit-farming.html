Rabbit Farming

Fast-Reproducing, Low-Cost Protein Complementing Sweet Potato Farming

Complete Nutrition Through Integration

While sweet potatoes provide essential vitamins and carbohydrates, protein is critical for fighting malnutrition. Rabbit farming perfectly complements our sweet potato programs by providing a reliable, affordable source of high-quality protein.

Rabbits reproduce quickly, require minimal space, and can be fed with sweet potato vines and other agricultural waste. They're quiet, clean, and ideal for small-scale family farming. A single breeding pair can produce dozens of offspring annually.

By integrating rabbit farming with sweet potato cultivation, families create a complete nutritional system - carbohydrates, protein, vitamins, and minerals - all from their own small farm while generating income from surplus production.

Why Rabbits?

  • High-quality lean protein for growing children
  • Reproduce rapidly - 40-60 offspring per year
  • Eat sweet potato vines and agricultural waste
  • Require minimal space and low startup cost
  • Manure fertilizes sweet potato fields

Integrated Farming System

1

Starter Breeding Pairs

Providing families with healthy breeding rabbits and training in proper care, housing, feeding, and breeding management.

2

Feed Integration

Teaching farmers to feed rabbits with sweet potato vines, creating a closed-loop system where nothing goes to waste.

3

Income & Nutrition

Families eat what they need and sell surplus rabbits for income, creating both nutritional security and economic opportunity.
