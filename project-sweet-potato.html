Sweet Potato Smart Farming

Combining Traditional Knowledge with Modern Technology to Fight Malnutrition

The Heart of GIFT's Mission

Sweet Potato Smart Farming is GIFT's flagship project, bringing together sustainable agriculture, appropriate technology, and community empowerment to address malnutrition and poverty in some of the world's most challenging environments.

Orange-fleshed sweet potatoes aren't just another crop - they're a nutritional powerhouse that can grow in difficult conditions, requires minimal inputs, and provides critical Vitamin A and nutrients that save children's lives.

By integrating soil sensors, weather monitoring, and data-driven farming practices, we help farmers maximize yields while preserving precious resources.

Why Sweet Potatoes?

  • Rich in Vitamin A - prevents childhood blindness and death
  • Grows in poor soils where other crops fail
  • Drought-resistant and climate-adaptable
  • Leaves are edible and highly nutritious
  • Fast-growing with good market value

How Smart Farming Works

1

Soil Sensors

IoT sensors monitor soil moisture, pH, and nutrients, helping farmers know exactly when to water and fertilize.

2

Weather Monitoring

Local weather stations provide real-time data on rainfall, temperature, and conditions to optimize planting and harvest times.

3

Training & Support

Farmers receive hands-on training in best practices, crop management, and using technology to improve yields.
