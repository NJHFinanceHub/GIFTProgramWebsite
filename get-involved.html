Impact of Your Support

$100

Starter Farm Kit

Provides a family with sweet potato vines, basic tools, and training to start their own sustainable farm.

$500

Farmer Training Program

Trains 10 farmers in sustainable sweet potato cultivation, including seeds, materials, and ongoing support.

$1,000

Technology Package

Installs soil sensors and weather monitoring equipment at a farm site, with data access for the community.

$5,000

Church Partnership

Establishes a full church twinning partnership with webcam, technology, training, and ongoing support for one year.

Every contribution makes a difference. Whether large or small, your support helps us fight hunger and poverty through sustainable agriculture.
